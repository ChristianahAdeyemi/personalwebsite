Meet Christianah

Hello there! I am Christianah Adeyemi, a Lawyer, Tech lover and Photographer! When asked how I combine all of these interests, I just let people know that I am fascinated by them and I find time to dedicate to their growth everyday. I am passionate about youth empowerment and believe constant self-development will aid me in imparting the knowledge I have to others.


My Vision

My vision is to one day start an organization where my interests can be merged and utilized in enhancing the state of the African society.

Gone are the days where women are afraid of the tech sector or afraid of chasing their dreams. Through my organization, I will inspire young people to venture into any sector they want, be it tech, law, photography or even something else, as long as it is in the advancement of the African people.
